How It Works

This site pairs a Cloudflare Worker API with a D1 database so your adventurers can enlist from anywhere.

1. Craft Your Token

Use Lexica Art to forge a striking profile portrait. Copy the image URL when you’re happy with it.

2. Link Your Character

Create or export your hero on D&D Beyond and copy the share link for your sheet.

3. Enlist Below

Submit the form to push your entry to the Cloudflare D1 roster. Your name, character, sheet link, and portrait will appear instantly.
